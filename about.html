Fire Stop Solution

we provide professional installation of fire stop sealants to prevent the spread of fire, smoke, and toxic gases through wall and floor penetrations. Our fire stopping solutions are essential for maintaining the integrity of fire-rated walls, slabs, and partitions around ducts, pipes, cables, and joints.
